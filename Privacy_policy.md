# Privacy Policy – Unsend

Last updated: March 2026

Unsend is designed to respect user privacy.

## Data Collection

Unsend does **not collect, store, or transmit any personal data**.

All messages written in the app remain **only on the user's device**.

The app does not use:
- analytics
- advertising trackers
- user accounts
- cloud storage

## Data Storage

Messages created in Unsend are stored locally on the user's device and automatically deleted based on the timer selected by the user.

The developers of Unsend do **not have access to user content**.

## Third-Party Services

Unsend does not use third-party services that collect personal data.

## Changes to This Policy

If the privacy policy changes, updates will be reflected on this page.
